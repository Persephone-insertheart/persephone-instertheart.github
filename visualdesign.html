Artifact 3

Artist statement

The way I made this project was with layer masking, The healing tool, scaling, and using other things that were made available to me through photopia. I decided to use light brown almond eyes, plumish lips that I sized down to look more like mine, and I used the nose and hair from the original picture. No, I personally don't think that this picture looks like me. I tried to make it real and they look exactly as I do but then when I realized it wasn't working out I remembered that not all of Picasso's pieces are really realistic so I decided to spice it up by adding an extra pair of lips and another eye in a different place.

Artifact 6

Artist statement

This project I named “The Ancient Dance of The Sea” Shows 5 dancers dancing along with the waves and motion that the sea gives. They are dancing to an ancient song of the aquatic drum in early 700bc hiding from historical civilization. I used the pen tool to draw the people and the paintbrush tool to add the water. The water isn't supposed to be as realistic for the fact that the story comes from an ancient mystical place.
